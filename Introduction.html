Introduction

What is data profiling?

The process of metadata discovery is called data profiling. It encompasses a vast array of methods to examine datasets and produce metadata. It is an important and frequent activity of any IT professional and researcher and is necessary for various use-cases.

blank

The motivation of learning data profiling

Data is everywhere, and it affects our daily life in every way. Data could be many types such as statistical, scientific and financial. Data profiling is the method of analyzing data. It is useful in helping understanding of data and improving data quality. There is no doubt that data profiling is becoming more and more important.
